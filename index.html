My name is Sana sanatan.I read in 6th standard. My hobby is Painting. Painting is the practice of applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface. The medium is commonly applied to the base with a brush, but other implements, such as knives, sponges, and airbrushes, can be used. In art, the term painting describes both the act and the result of the action.

Painting is one of those hobbies that gives you an opportunity to relax, enjoy and learn at the same time. We cannot imagine our life without colours and the essence of a painting lies in its colour scheme itself.
